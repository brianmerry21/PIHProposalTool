2. Executive Summary
Purpose: Propose an advanced storage solution using a Modula ML25 Vertical Lift Module (VLM) for a Thermo Fisher cleanroom in Hillsboro, OR.

Outcome: Maximize vertical space efficiency, increase storage capacity, and reduce operational inefficiencies​
.

3. Project Overview
VLM dimensions: 145.67" H x 177.83" W x 100.03" D.

Storage capacity: 634.66 sq ft of tray area, 363.6 cu ft of volume.

Includes 22 slotted trays, each capable of holding up to 551 lbs.

Designed with ISO7 cleanroom readiness and CE-standard safety features.

Equipped with Modula WMS Premium software for inventory and process optimization​
.

4. Why VLMs?
Benefits: Space savings, improved ergonomics, safety, productivity, and inventory accuracy.

Enables 24/7 inventory traceability and densified storage configurations​
.

5. Why Modula?
Modular, non-proprietary parts.

Ergonomic control panels and best-in-class anti-seismic features.

Made in the USA—compliant with "Buy America" standards​
.

6. Why PIH?
Leading Modula distributor in the Pacific Northwest.

Offers exclusive 3-scheduled maintenance visits (vs standard 1).

Includes factory-certified techs and $250K+ spare parts inventory​
.
